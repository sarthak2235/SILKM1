Bumthang Spiritual Valley

Ancient temples and sacred valleys at the heart of Bhutan.

Duration4 days

Max Altitude3,400 m

SeasonMar–May · Sep–Nov

DifficultyModerate

Overview

Bumthang is considered the spiritual heartland of Bhutan, containing some of the kingdom’s oldest temples and pilgrimage sites. The route blends cultural immersion with gentle valley walks.
