Bhutan Interior

Slow travel through Bhutan’s interior valleys and monasteries.

Duration10–14 days

Max Altitude4,500 m

SeasonMar–May · Sep–Nov

Overview

This journey explores Bhutan beyond its main corridors, focusing on interior valleys, sacred monasteries, and community-based travel. Routes emphasize cultural continuity, gradual elevation gain, and minimal environmental impact.
