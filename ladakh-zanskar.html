Zanskar Link Route

Remote valleys, dramatic gorges, and isolated Himalayan settlements.

Duration7 days

Max Altitude4,900 m

SeasonJuly – September

DifficultyChallenging

Overview

The Zanskar Link Route connects Ladakh with the rugged Zanskar region, known for deep gorges, river valleys, and cultural isolation shaped by geography.

Basic Itinerary

Day 1

Leh → Western Ladakh

Drive toward western valleys with gradual elevation changes.

Day 2

High Pass Crossing

Cross a major pass linking Ladakh and Zanskar regions.

Day 3

Zanskar Valley Exploration

Visit villages and monasteries along the valley floor.
