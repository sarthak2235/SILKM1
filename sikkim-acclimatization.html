Altitude Acclimatization Loop

Gradual elevation gain across Sikkim’s mountain corridors.

Duration3 days

Max Altitude3,800 m

SeasonMar – Jun · Sep – Nov

DifficultyModerate

Overview

This acclimatization loop is designed to prepare travelers for higher-altitude routes in North Sikkim. The journey emphasizes controlled elevation gain, rest periods, and monitoring of physiological response to altitude.

Basic Itinerary

Day 1

Gangtok → Mid-Altitude Region

Gradual ascent from Gangtok to a mid-altitude location. Focus on hydration, light walks, and rest.

Day 2

Elevation Gain & Short Walks

Short excursions to higher viewpoints followed by descent for overnight stay, reinforcing acclimatization.

Day 3

Return to Gangtok

Descend to Gangtok with stops for rest and monitoring. Evaluation for readiness to proceed to higher routes.

Detailed Itinerary & Budget

Recommended before visiting high-altitude destinations such as Lachen, Lachung, or Gurudongmar Lake.
