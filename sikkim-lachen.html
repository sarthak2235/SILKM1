Lachen & Gurudongmar Route

High-altitude lakes and Himalayan headwaters.

Duration3 days

Max Altitude5,430 m

SeasonApr – Jun · Sep – Nov

DifficultyHigh

Overview

This route leads to the sacred Gurudongmar Lake near the Indo–China border, passing through the alpine village of Lachen. Proper acclimatization is essential.

Basic Itinerary

Day 1

Gangtok → Lachen

Scenic drive with gradual altitude gain.

Day 2

Lachen → Gurudongmar Lake

Early morning visit to the lake followed by descent.

Day 3

Return to Gangtok

Drive back with rest stops.
