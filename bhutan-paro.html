Paro–Taktsang Circuit

Sacred monasteries and Bhutan’s iconic cliff temple.

Duration3 days

Max Altitude3,120 m

Overview

The Paro region hosts Bhutan’s most iconic landmarks, including Taktsang (Tiger’s Nest). This circuit blends hiking, history, and spiritual heritage.
