Nubra–Pangong Circuit

High-altitude deserts, alpine lakes, and iconic Himalayan passes.

Duration6 days

Max Altitude5,350 m

SeasonJune – September

DifficultyModerate–High

Overview

This circuit links the Nubra Valley with Pangong Lake via some of Ladakh’s most scenic high passes. The route contrasts sand dunes, glacial rivers, and vast turquoise lakes, while allowing gradual acclimatization through staged elevation gain.

Basic Itinerary

Day 1

Leh → Khardung La → Nubra Valley

Cross Khardung La and descend into the Nubra Valley. Evening rest and short acclimatization walks.

Day 2

Nubra Valley Exploration

Visit monasteries, sand dunes, and river confluences. Slow-paced exploration to aid acclimatization.

Day 3

Nubra → Shyok Route

Travel along the Shyok River corridor with changing desert and mountain landscapes.

Day 4

Pangong Lake

Full day around Pangong Lake focusing on short walks and rest near the lakeshore.

Detailed Itinerary & Budget

Route variations depend on road conditions and acclimatization. Contact us for a customized day-wise plan.
